Summary: Latitude vs. X


The purpose of this project is to analyze the correlation between various enviornmental factors as you get closer to the equator. To do this we pulled data from the OpenWeatherMapAPI to assemble a dataset for 500 cities.

After assembling the dataset, we used Matplotlib to visualize the results. The enviornmental factors we looked at are humidity, temperature, cloudiness, and wind speed. This website shows the source data, graphic visuals, and explanations of trends as they may appear.

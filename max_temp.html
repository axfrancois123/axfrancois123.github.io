Summary: Latitude vs. Max Temperature


According to our graph, we can see that the max temperature becomes higher as you approach the equator (zero latitude). The equator is the part of the Earth where there's more exposure to the sun. The countries that are closer to the equator are Colombia, Indonesia, Gabon,Somalia, Kenya, and many others. These countries will on average have a higher temperature than other countries of the data set.

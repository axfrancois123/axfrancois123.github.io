Summary: Latitude vs. Humidity


From the Latitude vs. Humidity graph visual, you can see that humidity reaches 100% more so around the latitude of -20 to around 60. Some of the countries that reside from latitude -20 to 60 are Brazil, Chile, Equador, Indonesia, Senegal, Madagascar, and many others. The majority of these countries have warmer climates and also reside near water.
We can also see that there are some small outliers around latitude 60 to 77 that have zero humidity. These countries are located closer to Antarctica which is why there's a lack of humidity.

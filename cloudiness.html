Summary: Latitude vs. Cloudiness


From the visulization shown on this page, you can see that there isn't much correlation between cloudiness and latitude in the southern hemisphere. I believe that is because the many of these countries in the data set are closer to the equator. These countries have a warmer tropical climate than that of the northern hemisphere which is why cloudiness doesn't correlate with latitude of our data set.
